The show revolves around the activity of the Sebben & Sebben law firm, which is staffed mainly by superheroes and other characters who had originally been featured in 1960s-era Hanna-Barbera cartoons, most notably Harvey Birdman (as the main character) and the Galaxy Trio. Harvey Birdman is a fictional superhero/attorney at law who first appeared on the Hanna-Barbera show Birdman and the Galaxy Trio as Ray Randall, Birdman. After returning as guest host in Space Ghost Coast to Coast as Harvey Birdman, he received a new spin-off solo series in Harvey Birdman, Attorney at Law, depicting his legal career. The character returned as United States Attorney General in Harvey Birdman: Attorney General.

DR. WEIRD

Dr. Weird is a supporting character and a main antagonist in the show Aqua Teen Hunger Force. For the first two seasons of the show, he was the subject of its cold openings. He rarely appears outside the cold openings. Dr. Weird wears a purple and yellow robe, with a W-shaped hole in the front. He also has a "hairarium" or hair heater around his head. He has white hair that somewhat forms the shape of a W. Reminiscent of super villains from the 1960s and 1970s, Dr. Weird wears a domino mask that covers his eyes, which appear to be solid red. He also wears gloves.
